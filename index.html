About

A writing app built with love.

Lekhini (లేఖిని) means "pen" in Telugu. It was built for writers who think in two languages, for people who want a private space to reflect, and for anyone who has ever wanted their words to look as beautiful as they feel.

It started as a personal project — a journal app I wished existed. Now it's growing into something more: a quiet corner of the internet for Telugu & English writers to write, design, and share.
